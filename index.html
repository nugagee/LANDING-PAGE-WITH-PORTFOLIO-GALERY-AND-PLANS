ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm theTechBoi.nugagee, a front-end web developer and also a content writer and creator. I'm based in Lagos, Nigeria. I have a passion for building beautiful websites for web and mobile devices. And have continuously developed my skills and knowledge as a developer over the years since. I made up my mind in taking my future in this direction. I love designing websites and keep things as simple as possible.

My goal is to focus on minimalism and conveying the message that you want to send. And also as a developer is to build pixel-perfect products that feel smooth and are scalable and efficient under the hood. As a developer, I know how to create your website to run across devices using the latest technologies available.

If you have any project in mind or if you want to build a website for your company or business, please kindly contact me or keep in touch with me at theTechBoi.nugagee@gmail.com or you can DMing me here on LinkedIn.
